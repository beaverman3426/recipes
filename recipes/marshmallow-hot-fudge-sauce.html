The Marshmallow Hot Fudge Sauce

Ingredients

  • 30 large marshmallows
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 12 ounce package semisweet choclate

Directions

  1. Heat IngredientsIn a saucepan over low heat, add marshmallows, milk, butter, and salt.
  2. Melt Marshmallows Stir constantly until the marshmallows melt (about 5 minutes).
  3. Melt Chocolate Keep stirring until the chocolate is fully melted and smooth (about 5 minutes).
  4. Serve Warm Pour into a cup or bowl and enjoy!
